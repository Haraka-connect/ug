About Us

Haraka Connect Ltd. is dedicated to providing convenient and affordable laundry services for all your clothing needs. Our goal is to make sure you have clean clothes without the hassle of doing laundry. From shirts to sneakers, we handle it all with care and professionalism.

With years of experience, our team ensures that your clothes are treated with the utmost care. Whether you’re a light user or need a heavy-duty plan, we offer flexible subscriptions tailored to meet your laundry needs.

Our Mission

  • Improve service accessibility by providing a reliable platform for customers to access services efficiently.
  • Establish partnerships with skilled professionals to ensure quality service delivery.
  • Monitor service processes to guarantee customer satisfaction and timely delivery.

FAQs

1. What services does Haraka Connect offer?

Haraka Connect provides a range of services connecting customers with skilled professionals in various fields, including plumbing, electrical work, cleaning, and more.

2. How can I request a service?

You can request a service by visiting our "Request Service" page, filling out the form, and providing details about your needs. Our team will get back to you promptly.

3. Are the professionals vetted?

Yes, all professionals are thoroughly vetted and undergo background checks to ensure quality and reliability.

. What is the response time for service requests?

We aim to respond to all service requests within 24 hours, depending on the availability of professionals in your area.

5. How can I pay for services?

Payment options will be provided upon service completion. We accept various methods, including cash and mobile money.

6. Do you offer guarantees on the services provided?

Yes, we offer a satisfaction guarantee on all services. If you're not satisfied, please contact us to resolve any issues.

7. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support via the "Contact Us" page or by calling our support line.
