Soap Making Training

About the Training

Join our comprehensive soap making training designed for both beginners and those with some experience in crafting soaps. Learn how to create high-quality bar and liquid soap using natural ingredients.

What You'll Learn

  • Ingredient Knowledge: Understand the various oils, fats, and lye required for soap making and their roles in the process.
  • Safety Practices: Learn essential safety measures to protect yourself while working with lye and other materials.
  • Techniques: Explore different methods such as cold process, hot process, and melt and pour techniques for soap making.
  • Creative Design: Discover how to add colors, scents, and textures to your soaps for a unique touch.
